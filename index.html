Privacy Policy

Soccer RadioTv Sync does not require users to create an account.

Soccer RadioTv Sync does not collect personal data, does not sell personal data, does not use advertising, does not use tracking, and does not use analytics or third-party SDKs for data collection.

The app allows users to play radio streams from URLs or playlists added by the user, and provides tools to adjust audio delay and synchronize radio playback with the TV picture.

The app uses an internet connection only to access radio streams provided by third-party URLs added by the user, and to check whether a stream URL is reachable.

Soccer RadioTv Sync does not store audio recordings.

Station URLs, playlists, language preferences and app settings may be stored locally on the user’s device only for the normal operation of the app.

Soccer RadioTv Sync does not provide radio stations or broadcast content. Users are responsible for the URLs and playlists they add, and should only use public or authorized sources.

If you have any questions about support or privacy, you can contact:
