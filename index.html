Privacy Policy

This app does not require account creation and does not sell personal data.

Soccer RadioTv Sync works as a player and synchronization tool for radio URLs and lists added by the user.

The app does not store audio recordings.

If you have any privacy-related questions, contact soccerradiotvsync@myyahoo.com.
